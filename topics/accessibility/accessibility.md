# Accessibility

- `Web Accessibility` means that people with disabilities can use the web
- It can be perceive, understand, navigate, interact and contribute
- The Goal is eliminate barriers that may prevent people with disabilities from interacting and accessing on the web.

### Dissabilities
- Visual
- Hearing
- Motor
- Neurological
- Speech
- Temporary
- Situational

Most of the people can have dissabilities when they are in `Temporary` or `Situational`

- Screen Reader
- Screen Magnifire
- Touch Screen Gestures
- Mouse or Pointers
